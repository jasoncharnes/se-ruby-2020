Southeast Ruby

We have fun bringing Ruby to the south.

In 2017 we introduced Southeast Ruby in Nashville, TN. In 2018 Ernie Miller joined us a co-organizer and we started to find our groove. In 2019 we moved the conference downtown and had one of the best years yet.

In 2020 we're taking a break.

We'll return in 2021 with a spectacular Southeast Ruby designed just for you!
